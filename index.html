ℹ️ AFNS admissions are announced once per year. Register on the official Pakistan Army website. Check joinpakarmy.gov.pk for latest updates.

✅ AFNS Eligibility Criteria

  • Gender: Females only
  • Nationality: Pakistani citizen (AJK/GB domicile also eligible)
  • Education: FSc Pre-Medical with minimum 50%
  • Hope Certificate accepted (FSc Part-I passed, Part-II appearing)
  • Age: 17–24 years (PRC) / 17–28 years (SSRC)
  • Height: Minimum 5 feet 2 inches
  • Unmarried at time of application
  • No dual nationality (must surrender on selection)

📋 Required Documents

  • Computerized CNIC or B-Form
  • FSc Certificate / Hope Certificate
  • Matric Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Crossed Postal Order Rs. 100 (in favor of DGPA, GHQ)
  • Character Certificate from institution

🎓 AFNS Training

  • 4-year BSc Nursing at AFPGMI Rawalpindi
  • Army pays ALL educational expenses
  • Monthly stipend during training
  • Guaranteed job in Pakistan Army after training
  • Two commission types: PRC (Permanent) & SSRC
AFNS Selection Process
Step-by-step joining procedure
1

Online Registration

Register at joinpakarmy.gov.pk/apply → OR visit nearest Army Selection & Recruitment Center (AS&RC)

2

Written / Intelligence Test

Initial test at AS&RC: General Science, Biology, Chemistry, English, IQ/Aptitude MCQs

3

Initial Medical Examination

Basic physical and medical check at AS&RC center

4

ISSB Test (4-Day)

Inter Services Selection Board test — intelligence, psychological, group tasks, interview. Shortlisted via AFNS official website + call-up letter.

5

Final Medical & GHQ Selection

Final comprehensive medical at CMH. GHQ prepares final merit list based on overall performance.

6

Training at AFPGMI Rawalpindi

4-year BSc Nursing with full army support, stipend, and guaranteed commission

✅ AMC Eligibility

  • Gender: Male (Medical Cadet) / Female via NUMS entry test
  • Army background: Not required (common misconception!)
  • Education: FSc Pre-Medical with minimum 60%
  • Age: 17–22 years
  • Pakistani nationality
  • Medically fit per Army standards
  • NUMS MDCAT eligible

💰 Fees & Benefits

  • Pakistan Army covers ALL educational expenses
  • Monthly stipend during MBBS/BDS
  • Commission as Captain Doctor after graduation
  • Minimum 17 years army service bond
  • World-class facilities at AMC Rawalpindi
  • Postgraduate options in army institutes

📋 Documents Required

  • CNIC / B-Form (candidate & parents)
  • FSc marks certificate / provisional
  • Matric certificate
  • Domicile certificate
  • Character certificate
  • 8 passport-size photographs
AMC Selection Process
Medical Cadet joining procedure
1

Online Application

Apply at joinpakarmy.gov.pk/apply → within the open registration window

2

NUMS Entry Test (MDCAT-based)

Biology, Chemistry, Physics, English MCQs. AMC uses NUMS test scores. Prepare via Noon Academy MDCAT track.

3

Initial Medical at AS&RC

Physical exam, height/weight check, vision test, dental screening

4

ISSB Test (4-Day)

Intelligence, psychological, group tasks, individual obstacles, final interview at ISSB center

5

Final Medical (CMH)

Comprehensive medical examination at Combined Military Hospital

6

GHQ Merit List & Training

Selected as Medical Cadet — MBBS/BDS at Army Medical College, Rawalpindi (NUMS affiliated)

Apply Now — joinpakarmy.gov.pk
