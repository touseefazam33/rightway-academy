Our Story

Born from a passion to serve Pakistani students

Rightway Academy was founded by Muhammad Touseef — a molecular biologist, bioinformatics researcher, and educator at Noon Academy — with a single goal: to make world-class education accessible to every student in Pakistan, regardless of their financial situation.

From free textbook access to live MCQ tests and professional MDCAT coaching, Rightway Academy combines the best of traditional teaching with modern ed-tech tools. We are proud to have helped 5,000+ students achieve top results in board exams and entry tests.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide free, high-quality educational resources and expert coaching to every Pakistani student.

🌟 Our Vision

To become Pakistan's most trusted online academy, recognized for results, integrity, and student welfare.
